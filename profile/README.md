# Routines

Routines is a social consistency platform built around one simple idea:

**Your daily actions should become visible proof of who you’re becoming.**

We believe routines are more than tasks and habits. They are repeated, intentional behaviors that shape identity over time. Whether it’s working out, reading, journaling, creating, or simply showing up for yourself, consistency deserves to be seen.

## Vision

Routines helps people:

- define the behaviors that matter to them
- show up every day with low-friction check-ins
- visualize consistency over time
- stay accountable through social visibility

At its core, Routines is about turning invisible effort into visible progress.

## What makes it different

Routines is not a generic to-do app.  
It is not just another habit tracker.  
It is not a noisy social network.

It is a product focused on:

- recurring, identity-driven behavior
- visual consistency
- accountability through shared progress
- building momentum, not chasing perfection

## Core belief

People are more likely to stay consistent when their progress is:

- visible
- cumulative
- meaningful
- socially legible

## In one line

**Routines helps people build identity through visible consistency.**
